MvM

Since 1992, VZW Moeders voor Moeders has been committed to the poorest people in Flanders. More than 160 volunteers distribute materials and food to families with young children who are struggling financially. Every week, our non-profit organisation distributes food parcels to hundreds of families. In addition to food, mothers can obtain all kinds of material assistance for their children, such as children's clothes, care materials, baby equipment, etc. The non-profit organisation also provides various services, such as the resto-bébé, where the little ones can have a bath and a hot meal twice a week.

Volunteer: Something for you?

In our non-profit organisation, 160 volunteers are active in more than 20 departments. Everyone works for free in the department that suits them best. The work mainly involves sorting, processing and distributing clothing, food, baby material, etc. In addition, we are also looking for volunteers for services such as the resto, transport, maintenance, receiving materials ... Moeders voor Moeders is only open on Tuesdays and Thursdays. All our volunteers come to help on one of these days. Can you free up one day a week to lend a hand in one of our departments? We are happy to give you a tour to get to know our organisation. Send us an e-mail (info@moedersvoormoeders.be), leave your details with us contactformulier or contact us by phone preferably on Tuesday or Thursday on the number 03/272.53.84.

You can support us

Our non-profit organisation is a private organisation and is not subsidised. We survive only thanks to financial and material donations from all over the country. All our volunteers work on an unpaid basis.

  • Material

    You can support Moeders voor Moeders with food and materials that we distribute to our families (our needs)

    If you want to set up a collection campaign with your company or association, please contact us in advance.

  • Financial
    You can support Moeders voor Moeders financially in various ways. our non-profit organisation is authorised to issue tax certificates for donations of 40 euros or more on an annual basis.
    • Any donation is welcome on the number BE90 4043 0997 1232 (BIC: KREDBEBB)
    • You can also let Moeders voor Moeders share in your celebrations. Are you celebrating a birth, birthday, wedding, anniversary or retirement? You can support us in two ways:
      1. Your guests deposit the donation into our account with a fixed notice chosen by you. We personally thank each donor and keep you regularly informed of the donations you have received. Please contact us in advance to make practical arrangements for this.
      2. Your guests make a direct donation to you, after which you transfer an amount to us, or buy products with us. In this case, we cannot provide a tax receipt for your guests.
  • Adopt a "boeleke"
    In order to guarantee the continued operation of our resto-bébé, we are permanently looking for families who would like to symbolically adopt a little tree. With a monthly contribution of €12, you can help ensure the future of this service. For more information, please see our leaflet request or download.
  • Bequest / duo bequest

    You can include Moeders voor Moeders in your will via a bequest or duo bequest. As a charity, VZW Moeders voor Moeders pays only 8.8% inheritance tax. With a duo bequest, you support a charity, while your heirs inherit at least the same or even more. For more information, please contact your notary or you can download our brochure request or download.

  • Action in favour of Moeders voor Moeders
    If you want to set up an action to collect money for our non-profit organisation, we ask you to contact us in advance to discuss the modalitites. Please do not organise any fundraising activities for us without prio consulation.

For further information, please contact us at contact form, e-mail: info@moedersvoormoeders.be or by telephone on the number: 03 / 272 53 84 (preferably on Tuesdays or Thursdays).

Our needs

Individuals

In order to meet the constant demand, we need children's clothes up to the age of 14, all kinds of baby material, toys in very good condition and completely intact, care products, bath and bed linen, children's shoes, school equipment, maternity clothes and christening sugar all year round. Only material and clothing in clean and good condition will be accepted. In addition, we can use all kinds of perishable food to include in our food parcels. Old caddies and luggage racks are also very welcome. Because of the limited possibilities, we cannot come and collect materials at home.

Companies

May we also appeal to shops and manufacturers to deliver to us end-of-series items, obsolete samples, overstocks or products that cannot be sold due to circumstances. We also have a permanent need for packaging materials and large (reusable) carrier bags. Our non-profit organisation has a transport service with vans to collect goods.

Collection campaigns

May we ask you to contact us beforehand, if you want to set up a collection with your association or company. Then we can inform you of our most urgent needs. Please do not organise a toy collection without prior consultation.

Route description


  • By car
    • The Helmstraat is a side street of the Turnhoutsebaan/Carnostraat (main entrance road from the Singel - exit Borgerhout towards Central Station and Zoo) About 1 kilometre on your right you will find the Helmstraat. Moeders voor Moeders is located approximately 500 metres on your left.
  • By public transport
    • From the central station (A), you need to walk about 15 minutes. You can also take a tram or bus from Koningin Astridplein. The tram and bus stop where you need to get off is called "Kerkstraat".
    • Bus lines 23, 450 4xx
    • Tramlines 10 en 24
